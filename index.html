What is Aeroponics?

Aeroponis is the process of cultivating plants in an air or mist environment, eliminating the need for soil or an aggregate medium. The term "aeroponic" orginates from the Greekwords aer ("air") and ponos ("work")......Studies have shown that plants grown aeroponically can grow five times faster than plants grown in soil. The Aeroponic System is an active system that's suitable for advanced growers. It's also one of the more expensive and complex options in terms of setup. In this system, plants are suspended - usually in net pots - while their roots are continuously misted with nutrient solution through the use of a pump and timer..

How does Aeroponic grow?

Aeroponics is a method of growing plants without soil. Instead roots are suspended in the air and irrigated with a nutrient-dense mist. This differs from hydroponics, where plant roots are submerged in a solition of water and nutrients.

Why choose Aeroponics?

  • Higher growth rates create a quicker turnover of your product.
  • Greater control of nutrient application enables you to consistently tune crop quallity.
  • Because mist is applied only to the root zone, the growing medium remains dry, so there is less chance of losing profit to pests or disease.
  • Relaible, year-round harvests mean you can have consistent sales projections and estimates.
